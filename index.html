Here's Who We Are:

Jake McKelvie tours around the country both with The Countertops and as a solo act. Having self-released numerous full-length albums and EPs over the years, he has carved himself a niche as a songwriter, "combining a rapid delivery with stream-of-self-consciousness writing in a manner so singular that it is never in question whose songs are being played."
